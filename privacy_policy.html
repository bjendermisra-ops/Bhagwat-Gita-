Hare Krishna! Welcome to the premium Srimad Bhagavad Gita App. We deeply respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data [2]. This Privacy Policy document outlines our transparent practices regarding data collection, native Android frameworks, and local storage mechanisms, ensuring complete compliance with the Google Play Developer Guidelines [1, 2].

1. Information Collection & Usage

This mobile application is built on a strict "No Forced Login" models [2]. You can access all features, including daily shlokas, bookmarks, and language translations, without creating an account or submitting any personal details [2].

A. Complete Local Device Storage:

To provide a seamless, highly customizable spiritual experience, we save all your progress data locally on your device storage (using SharedPreferences and sandboxed database systems of the mobile OS). This data is NEVER transmitted or uploaded to our external servers [2]. This includes:

  • Daily streak tracking and historical reading records [5].
  • Earned Karma Points for completing spiritual verses.
  • Saved bookmarks of your favorite verses.
  • UI theme preferences (Night, Day, and Eye Protection modes) and selected active languages (Hindi, Marathi, English).
B. Zero Personal Data Transmission:

Because we do not use registration systems, we do not collect or request sensitive personal details such as names, phone numbers, location, contacts, or credit card details [2]. Your spiritual progress data remains completely under your own physical control [2].

2. Native Device Permissions

To function efficiently and provide a premium user experience, the application utilizes standard device permissions. Below is the detailed explanation for complete transparency as required by Google Play Console [1]:

Android Scoped Storage & MediaStore API

Our application features a "Shloka Story Mode" which lets you generate highly customized, beautifully illustrated shlok cards to put on your Instagram or WhatsApp status. To facilitate this securely:

  • We comply fully with Android's modern Scoped Storage guidelines [3].
  • We use the native MediaStore API to write the customized story image directly into your public Downloads directory [3].
  • By adhering to these modern standards, our app does not require any legacy, intrusive read/write storage permissions on modern Android versions, safeguarding your private files [3].
  • Internet Access: Required to fetch dynamic translations, download chapter files, load custom Sanskrit fonts, and stream daily chanting audio files from our secure cloud services [4].
  • Access Network State: Required to detect active device connectivity [4]. This check prevents unexpected interface freezes or app crashes when your phone loses network coverage.
  • Foreground Service & Sleep Prevention: Used strictly to keep the screen responsive while you are actively contemplating on the divine translations or listening to continuous audios.

3. External Intent Redirections

Our app includes deep links to our official social media channels (YouTube, Facebook, Instagram) and auxiliary play store utilities. Clicking these links triggers native system intents that securely launch the official external applications on your device outside our sandbox.

Please be aware that your interactions and data on these external platforms are strictly governed by the Privacy Policies and Terms of Service of those respective third-party networks.

4. Third-Party Web Technologies & Advertising

To support application development and server maintenance costs, we may integrate trusted third-party services and advertising networks. These services may collect basic device identifiers according to their respective policies:

  • Advertising Networks (AdMob, Unity, Start.io): Our application may display ads. These integrated networks use your device's Advertising ID and network headers to serve relevant ads without exposing any personally identifiable information (PII).
  • Privacy Sandbox & AdServices APIs: In compliance with modern Android SDKs, we utilize non-intrusive technologies such as the AdServices Topics API, attribution APIs, and Play Install Referrer API. This enables secure, anonymous performance measurement without tracking user identity. You can manage or reset your Advertising ID settings anytime through your device's Google settings.

5. Children & Family Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

The spiritual teachings of Srimad Bhagavad Gita are moral, educational, and completely safe for all audiences, including children. In strict accordance with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA), we declare that we do not knowingly request, collect, or store personal information from children under the age of 13.

6. Data Retention & Deletion Policy

Since our application operates on a zero-account model, we do not maintain persistent user profiles or cloud data to delete [5]. You have full control over your localized progress data, streaks, and bookmarks:

  • You can clear all app progress, cache, and saved bookmarks at any time by navigating to your device's Settings -> Apps -> Bhagavad Gita App -> Storage and selecting "Clear Data" [5].
  • Uninstalling the application will automatically and permanently delete all sandboxed progress files from your device hardware [5].
How to Request Data Deletion (Play Store Requirement):

For any data deletion inquiries or questions regarding your local privacy, you can directly email our administration team at support@bhagavadgitaapp.com [5]. Verifiable requests will be addressed promptly.

7. GDPR & CCPA Compliance Disclosures
